WE STAND FOR

An easier way to get the problems heard. Problems ranging from small to massive comprising Electricity Cut-off, Sewage Cleaning, Lack of transportation, Unusual Activities Complaint and a lot more.

SmartFolks offer you a 3 tier platform to make your life easier and better and smarter. The 3 tier includes FOLKS, ADMINISTRATION, ORGANIZATION

FOLKS are you people out there facing day to day problems and having tough times throughout the day. Now get your problems heard by the concerned department or authority and let them know what problems we as common people face in our daily lives. The idea of SmartFolks is not to initiate any immediate action on the problems instead focuses on making concerned aware of the problems out there. Register @SmartFolks and support the problems uploaded by people of your region. You can upload your problem using create post feature. You can also follow various organization of your region or outer and get their activities known.

ADMINISTRATION this includes concerned authorities in the SmartFolks platform to hear the problems of the concerned regions and take them into consideration. SmartFolks will itself try to add as many concerned authorities as it can and authorities can also join themselves the platform by contacting SmartFolks. After joining the platform, an authority can view various problems of its concerned region and provide required actions or steps to curb a particular problem.

ORGANIZATION is an additional and interesting feature added to the platform to provide you with a number of events happening in your region. Organization includes firms, institutions, NGOs, Government bodies etc which will post their events on the platform. SmartFolks can either contact organization to come onboard to the platform or organizations can contact SmartFolks directly.
